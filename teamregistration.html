FAQ

Can I register for a team which doesn't have a complete roster?

Yes, an incomplete team registration will be submitted and will later be matched with other incomplete members

Why can't I specify my position/role in the team registration?

The team registration only registers members of team on level. The team details and positions are determined by the members when the teams have been finalised

Why can't I enter team name?

Team names can be entered if you register with a complete team. Incomplete teams must be matched with other members and team names can then be decided
